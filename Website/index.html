About Us

We are an interdisciplinary club interested in learning and teaching all of the aspects of making a game. The types of games include, but are not limited to, computer games, board games, card games, and role play games. Our focus will be on educating and discussing the elements that goes into creating high quality games as well as the resources necessary, and how to combine talents from various disciplines together to make a successful game.
